### **About the Artist**

*Abstract Art for sale, directly from the artist Ronald Hunter. Welcome to the official Ronald Hunter art gallery. Discover the large collection of [affordable abstract](https://ronaldhunter.com/affordable-paintings/), modern & pop art paintings from this Dutch online art gallery in Rotterdam. World Wide Shipping, Free 14 day full refund policy. Safe & secure payment methods.*

Ronald Hunter is a [Dutch artist](https://ronaldhunter.com/the-artist/)/ entrepreneur who is known for his large abstract paintings. He wants to make original art [available for everyone](https://ronaldhunter.com/affordable-paintings/). The bright and light colorful artworks will fit any kind of environment. Ronald Hunter paintings are sold to private residences and office spaces. His art is in private and public collections in over 52 countries. Ronald’s art is also often used by interior design agencies who are looking for a business partner for their [professional projects](https://ronaldhunter.com/custom-art/).
